Design and build BatchKart (batchkart.com).

BatchKart is India's batch discovery platform where students can discover coaching batches, compare prices, receive discounts, request recommendations, and connect with coaching institutes.

The product should feel like a modern startup (similar quality to Airbnb, Stripe Dashboard, Notion, Linear, and Framer).

The design must be clean, minimal, premium, extremely fast, and SEO optimized.

Goal

Create India's largest coaching batch marketplace.

Students should be able to

Discover coaching batches
Compare coaching institutes
Get exclusive discounts
Request personalized batch recommendations
Post their requirements
Receive suggestions from coaching institutes

Feature-based organization is preferred over large generic folders.
